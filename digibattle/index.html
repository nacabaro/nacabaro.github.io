Digibattle!!

Welcome to DigiBattle, a proof of concept that generates a random Digimon you can battle on your Digimon V-Pet by making use of an ACom/DCom device.

Note: This website only works on Google Chrome and Chromium derivates, as the JavaScript Serial API is still in beta and has not been released to Firefox. Sorry for the inconvenience!

To get started, connect your ACom/DCom to your computer, and click on the ATTACH A-COM icon!, then select your A-Com from the list of serial devices and everything should be ready.
